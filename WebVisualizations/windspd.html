Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The data does not appear to reveal any meaningful relationship between latitude and wind speed. Local winds are influenced by global wind patterns and regional weather phenomena, which depend on both latitude and longitude. All of the analyses performed here only considered latitudinal coordinates, which we have demonstrated here is likely not sufficient for accurately modeling wind speeds.
